My Trip to Florida

Universal Studios

Back in Summer of 2016, a group of friends and I traveled to Florida to enjoy our summer,

by the gate was a giant fake wave, and it was a perfect photo op,

especially for photos like this...

There were many places to visit and rides to go on,

such as Jurassic Park...

and Skull Island.

The most notable place of all, however, was Diagon Valley from Harry Potter

Where the Gringott's Dragon breathes its fire among the visitors.

We managed to time the interval of its fire breathing and got some great footages.

We had a rather short stay at the studios, but nevertheless it was a fun visit.

Disneyland

For the second part of our trip we went to Disneyland. As I took this photo i realized that using with a bit of editing this could totally be a evil castle.

Ditto.

For the most part we simply walked around and enjoyed the place, but I did ended up meeting Pooh during dinner in Disney.

And Eeyore.

To be honest we were just really excited for the lightshow at night.

I'll have to admit that I've a new found love for Tinkerbell.

Departure

Vacation time eventually came to an end, but we all look forward to the next time we travel together.
